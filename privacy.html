Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

The short version: Marg doesn't collect any personal data. Everything you add to the app — quotes, words and books — is stored only on your own device and never leaves your phone.

Who we are

Marg is an app made by Margapp, an independent developer project. The app is available on Google Play and is designed as a reading companion for people who want to save quotes and words while they read.

What data do we collect?

We don't collect any personal data. Marg has no:

  • User accounts or login
  • Servers that receive data from your app
  • Analytics or tracking tools
  • Advertising or third-party data collection
  • Access to contacts, calendar, microphone or other sensitive permissions

What is stored on your device?

All content you create in Marg is stored locally on your phone using the device's standard storage mechanism:

  • Quotes you have saved
  • Words from the Norwegian dictionary you have saved
  • Books you have added to your shelf
This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it using the app's export function. You have full control over your data at all times.

Camera access

Marg requests access to the camera if you use the scanning feature to photograph book pages. Photos taken are used only for text recognition on the device itself — they are not stored, not sent to any server, and not used for any other purpose.

Internet access

The app uses the internet for two things:

  • Fetching dictionary data from ordbokapi.org (the Norwegian dictionary) when you search for a word
  • Fetching book information from Open Library when you scan an ISBN or search for a book

These are publicly available services and receive only the search terms you type. We have no affiliation with them beyond standard API usage.

Third-party libraries

Marg uses the following third-party library for on-device text recognition:

  • Google ML Kit (text recognition) — runs locally on your device, no data is sent to Google

Children

Marg does not collect data from any users, including children. The app is not specifically directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, the updated version will be published on this page with a new date. Since Marg doesn't collect data, this is unlikely to change significantly.

Questions?

If you have any questions about privacy in Marg, feel free to get in touch: hello@margapp.no
